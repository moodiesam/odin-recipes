Salami Cotto

Salami Cotto covers all the bases.

Salami Cotto is a beautiful cold cut to make at home. Salty with a nice peppery kick, it is perfect for a sandwich, pizza, or charcuterie board!

Ingredients

  • 3x Collagen Casings
  • 12-lbs Pork trim, 20% Fat
  • 148g Kosher Salt
  • 4g Pink Salt
  • 32g Fennel Seeds, Cracked
  • 16g Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • 12g Fresh Garlic, Minced
  • 8g Fresh Rosemary, Finely Choped
  • 60g Black Pepper, Finely Ground

Steps

  1. Soak the Collagen Casings in warm water
  2. Grind the Pork Trim through the large grinder plate
  3. Combine all the ground pork with the rest of the ingredients and mix in a stand mixer until tacky and well incorperated
  4. Using a sausage stuffer, stuff the mixture into the casings
  5. Let hang overnight
  6. The next day, steam the salami at 200F until it reaches an internal temperature of 155F
  7. Immediately chill in an ice bath and then hang until the next day
  8. Slice thin and enjoy!
