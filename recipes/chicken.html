Buttermilk Chicken

Deliciously tender grilled chicken thighs

This recipe will become the only chicken you want to make in the summer. Deliciously tender with just the right ammount of heat and sweetness. A crowd pleaser to say the least!

Ingredients

  • 5-lbs Boneless, Skinless Chicken thighs
  • 1-L Buttermilk
  • 2-Tbsp Kosher Salt
  • 1-Tbsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1-Tbsp Ground Cumin
  • 1-tsp Granulated Garlic
  • 1-tsp Mustard Powder
  • 1-tsp Ground Black Pepper
  • 1/2-tsp Onion Powder

Steps

  1. Combine the Salt, Paprika, Cumin, Garlic, Mustard Powder, Pepper and Onion Powder in a small bowl
  2. In a large bowl, season the Chicken Thighs with the spice mixture
  3. Mix well to destribute the seasoning evenly
  4. Pour the Buttermilk over the chicken. Be sure to move the chicken around enough enough to let the buttermilk coat all of the chicken
  5. Cover and refrigerate overnight
  6. The next day, preheat the grill for direct, medium heat
  7. Place the chicken thighs onto the grill directly from the buttermilk, allowing any excess to drip off
  8. Grill the chicken thighs until done (about 7 minutes per side)
  9. Let rest for five minutes
  10. Tuck in!
